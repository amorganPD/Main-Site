Random Dungeons
3D Browser Based Game
Random Dungeons - added 2015-04-25
One of my first, more complete, attempts at a video game. This project is still in development and is a completely browser based application using BabylonJS as the webGL engine. All the 3D and 2D assets were created by me using Blender and photoshop. Primarily focusing on using the BabylonJS framework, pure JavaScript is heavily used to create the game logic and to tie into BJS together.
Laser Tag
Personal Project
added 2015-04-25
I first started working on this in 2013. It was for educational experience for surfacing, plastic components, which were 3D printed, embedded hardware and software design. A netduino was used at the time for quick development, but soon learned the limitations, aka the lack of support for common components. Working with one of my friends (Ghost) we made a base station that had a IR detector with a netduino and on the other end the laser tag gun with an IR emitter and a 7-segment display to display the ammo. In all it was a pretty simple design and hoped to expand off of it, but as things usually go, other projects, not enough time. I hope to get back to this and make a complete project. Keep in tune.

Offroad Electric Vehicle
CAR Technologies LLC
added 2015-04-25
This is a concept that was developed while I was at CAR Technologies LLC. The suspension and frame were designed referencing offroad Pro 2 trucks. Part of this design was conceptual, part of it was mechanically designed, such as the frame and suspension. With the help of a suspension design tool, the upper and lower A-arms, knuckle, upper and lower shock mounting were designed. In addition to the mechanical concept, the electric powertrain was also packaged and specified from a systems design perspective.
