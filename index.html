Embedded Projects

Laser Tag
Personal Project
added 2015-04-25
I first started working on this in 2013. It was for educational experience for surfacing, plastic components, which were 3D printed, embedded hardware and software design. A netduino was used at the time for quick development, but soon learned the limitations, aka the lack of support for common components. Working with one of my friends (Ghost) we made a base station that had a IR detector with a netduino and on the other end the laser tag gun with an IR emitter and a 7-segment display to display the ammo. In all it was a pretty simple design and hoped to expand off of it, but as things usually go, other projects, not enough time. I hope to get back to this and make a complete project.
